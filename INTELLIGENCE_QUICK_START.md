## How to Use

### 1. Start Chatting
Just use the app normally. The system learns automatically!

### 2. View Your Profile
Click the **User icon** in the header to see:
- What the AI has learned about you
- Your favorite topics
- Your communication preferences
- Learning statistics

### 3. Analyze Conversations
Click **"Analyze All"** in your profile to process all existing conversations and build your intelligence profile faster.

### 4. Reset if Needed
Click **"Reset"** in your profile to clear all learned data and start fresh.
